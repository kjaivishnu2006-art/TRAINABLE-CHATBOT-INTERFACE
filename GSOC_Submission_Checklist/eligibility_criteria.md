# Eligibility Criteria for GSoC Contributors

To participate in Google Summer of Code, you must meet the following requirements:

## 1. Age
- You must be 18 years of age or older upon registration.

## 2. Background
- You must be a student or a beginner to open-source software development.
- Beginners are defined as individuals with minimal experience in open-source contributing (e.g., less than a year of activity).

## 3. Location & Work Status
- You must be eligible to work in your country of residence for the duration of the program.
- You must NOT be a resident of a U.S. embargoed country.

## 4. Prior Participation
- You must NOT have participated as a GSoC contributor more than once previously. (Maximum two lifetime participations).

## 5. Conflict of Interest
- You cannot be a GSoC Mentor or Organization Administrator for the current year.

> [!NOTE]
> GSoC is no longer limited to university students. Professionals, boot camp graduates, and self-taught developers are all welcome to apply as "beginners."
